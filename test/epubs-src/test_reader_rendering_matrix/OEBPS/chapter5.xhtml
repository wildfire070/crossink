Entities and Unicode

Named entities: ampersand &, less-than <, greater-than >, nonbreaking space A B, middle dot ·, em dash —, curly quotes “quoted”, and apostrophe ’.

Accented and non-English text: cafe, café, naive, jalapeno, facade, Sao Paulo, Krakow, Zurich. These should render or fall back without corrupting the line.
