Should Stay Unchanged

PASS with Force Paragraph Indents either OFF or ON: the centered and right-aligned samples below should not suddenly gain a left-style paragraph indent.

Centered paragraph

Centered text should remain centered, without the first line being shoved inward like a normal left-aligned paragraph.

Right-aligned paragraph

Right-aligned text should stay right-aligned, again with no added left-style first-line indent.

Negative hanging indent

Bullet style content should keep its hanging shape. The marker line should hang out to the left instead of being flattened into a normal paragraph.

A second hanging paragraph makes it obvious if the structure gets broken by the setting.
